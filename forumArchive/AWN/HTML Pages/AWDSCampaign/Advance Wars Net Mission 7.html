Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Advance Wars DS - Normal Campaign

Mission 07: Fog Rolls In

Difficulty: **
Days: 7
Armies: OS (Sasha) vs BH (Koal)
Walkthrough: 5-days 300pts S-rank
OS Units [9]: Infantry, Mech, APC, Recon, Tank, Anti-Air, Artillery, Rocket, B-Copter
BH Units [9]: Infantry, Mech, APC, Recon, Tank (2), Artillery (2), Lander 
OS Buildings [3]: City (2), HQ
BH Buildings [3]: City (2), HQ
Neutral [2]: City (2)

This map presents you the first Fog of War battle in Campaign. Remember the basics about FoW: be careful with units hiding in woods and reefs, keep your units together and protect your Recon. The enemy has no anti-aerial units, so your B-Copter is safe. And finally, if you want to win by rout, don't forget to check the single reef tile.

First, you have to deal with the Artillery that's hiding in the woods. The only units that are a threat in this mission are the two tanks, so destroy them as soon as possible. Use your B-Copter and tank to attack them; bait them with your Infantry or APC. It's also a good idea to move your Recon forward (hide it in woods) in turn one to spy on the enemy units; don't forget that if you move it to another wood tile while in the enemy vision range, they will know where your Recon is and go after it!

» Day 1

Orange Star:

Move the Recon 5E, and the Infantry 2E to reveal a hidden unit. Move the B-Copter 1N 5E and attack the Artillery. Load the Mech into the APC, move it 2S 3E and drop the soldier to the right. Move the Artillery 2S 3E, the Tank 4S 2E, Rocket 1S 3E and Anti-Air 4S 2E.

Black Hole:

Units will move forward. APC will retreat, Recon will move in and allow the Artillery to fire in your Anti-Air, and an Infantry will begin to capture one of your cities.

» Day 2

Orange Star:

Move your B-Copter 6E. Move the Mech 1E and destroy the Artillery. Move the Tank 2E 1N 1E and attack the Recon. Move your Infantry 3E to bait the enemy Tank and begin capturing the city. Move the AA 1S 3E and destroy the Recon. Move the Artillery 2S 2E, Rocket 4E, APC 2S 2E 1N and Recon 2W 2N.

Black Hole:

Units will move and capture. The visible Tank will reveal your Recon and another Tank will come in and attack it.

» Day 3

Orange Star:

Attack the south Tank with your Rocket. Move the Anti-Air 5E and attack the damaged Tank, then move the Infantry 2E and destroy it. Move the B-Copter 2N 1W and attack the healthy Tank, then move your Tank 2N 3W and destroy it too. Move the Artillery 3E 2N, and the Recon 1E 5N. Load the Mech into the APC, move it 3E 3N and drop the Mech to the north. Koal's SCOP should not be charged yet.

Black Hole:

The Mech will finish capturing your city, and the Infantry will attack your Mech.

» Day 4

Orange Star:

Move the Anti-Air 5N and attack the Infantry, then move your Infantry 3N and kill it. Move the Artillery 1N and the Rocket 1E 2N. Move the B-Copter 2N 2E and attack the Mech, then move the Tank 3E 1N and kill it too. Load the Mech into the APC again, move it 1S 3E and drop the Mech to the left. Move the Recon 2S and attack the APC.

Black Hole:

The surviving Lander and Artillery will hide in woods and reefs.

» Day 5

Orange Star:

Use your CO Power. Move the Recon 1N 1W to reveal the Artillery, and the APC 4W 1N to reveal the Lander. Attack and destroy the Lander with both your Rocket and Artillery. Move the Mech 1N to attack the APC, then the Anti-Air 1E, and finally the Infantry 1S 2E to destroy it. Move the Tank 1W 3N and attack the Artillery, and finish it off with your B-Copter.
