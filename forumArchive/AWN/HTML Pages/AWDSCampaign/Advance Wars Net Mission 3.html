Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Advance Wars DS - Normal Campaign

Mission 03: Max Attacks

Difficulty: *
Days: 8
Armies: OS (Max) vs BH (Lash)
Walkthrough: 5-days 300pts S-rank



OS Units [10]: Mech (2), APC, Artillery, T-Copter, B-Copter(5)
BH Units [7]: Infantry (2), Tank (2), Md Tank (2), Black Bomb

OS Buildings [2]: City, HQ
BH Buildings [2]: City, HQ
Neutral: -

You need a little more strategy to win here, but the map is still very easy. Let the copters take the Black Bomb fire and position your troops to stop the enemy units from crossing the bridge. Send one of the Mechs in the T-Copter to capture the enemy HQ.

In the first turn, you will have to spread your B-Copters around so you take minimal damage from the Black Bomb's explosion, which does 5HP of damage in a wide range. Then move the B-Copters to hold the bridge: the Md Tanks will not be able to break through. The Artillery is weaker under Max's command, but it will be enough to attack the enemy troops from afar. Join the damaged copters if needed. To get a perfect Power score all you have to do is destroy at least one enemy unit. You may lose up to two units while keeping a perfect Technique score.

» Day 1

Orange Star:

Move the eastern Mech 2W, and the Artillery 1W. Move the T-Copter 4W 2S, then move the APC 1W. Move the B-Copters, from top to bottom, in this order: 2N 4W, 3S 3W, 6W, 6W, and 1N 5W.

Black Hole:

Lash will detonate the Black Bomb close to the B-Copters formation, thus sparing your Mech. The other troops will be moved forward.

» Day 2

Orange Star:

Load the left Mech into the T-Copter. Move the T-Copter 4S 2W. Move the healthy B-Copter 2N 1W. Move the damaged B-Copters, from left to right, in this order: 4N 2E, 5N 1E, 6N (join), 5N 1W (join). Move the Artillery 1W, the APC 2W and the remaining Mech 2W.

Black Hole:

Troops will be moved forward again. Md Tanks will face your B-Copters but they may not attack.

» Day 3

Orange Star:

Move the T-Copter 3S 2W and drop the Mech to the left. Use the Artillery to attack the Md Tank in range. Move the bottom B-Copter 1N and attack the Tank (if the Tank is not there, move to an adjacent tile to it and attack). Use the middle B-Copter to attack the healthy Md Tank.

Black Hole:

Units will move around.

» Day 4

Orange Star:

Begin to capture the enemy HQ with your Mech. Use the Artillery to attack whatever is in range, then use the B-Copter left to it to destroy the damaged unit. Use the middle B-Copter to attack an enemy unit without moving, if possible.

Black Hole:

Units will move around... again.

» Day 5

Orange Star:

Finish capturing the HQ.

Victory!
Speed: 100pts 5 days
Power: 100pts 2 of 7 units destroyed in a single day
Technique: 100pts 0 of 10 units lost

300pts S-rank!
