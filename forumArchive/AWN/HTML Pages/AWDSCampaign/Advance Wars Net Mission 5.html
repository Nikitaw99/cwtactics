Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Advance Wars DS - Normal Campaign

Mission 05: Neverending War

Difficulty: **
Days: 9
Armies: OS [Jake] vs BH (Koal)
Walkthrough: 8-days 300pts S-rank
OS Units [2]: Mech (2)
BH Units [7]: Mech (2), Recon, Artillery, Anti-Air, Tank, Md Tank
OS Buildings [7]: City (4), Base (2), HQ
BH Buildings [5]: City (4), HQ
Neutral [5]: City (4), Base

This is the first map that actually requires a strategy. You will need to avoid the first units Koal sends at you, then bait in the Md Tank and destroy it with an Artillery (since you cannot buy the more expensive units). Therefore Max is a bad idea; choose Jake as your CO here.

First, build a Tank in the north base. It will scare the weaker vehicles away and they'll move south. Then, build a Tank south to deal with them, while baiting the southern Tank with your Mech. Then, in the north bases, build an Artillery and move it so it can attack the Md Tank later, and a Tank, to switch with the Tank that will be damaged by the Mid's attack. Be careful with Koal's power, as the extra movement can break a well-thought strategy.

» Day 1

Orange Star:

Move the north Mech 2E, and the south one 2S. Build a Tank in the northern base.

Black Hole:

Recon and Anti-Air will move southwest, Mechs will be moved forward.

» Day 2

Orange Star:

Move the Tank 1N 5E. Move the south Mech 2E to bait the enemy Tank, and deploy a Tank in the south base.

Black Hole:

The Tank will attack your Mech. Recon & AA will keep moving south.

» Day 3

Orange Star:

Move south Mech 1S 1E and attack the Tank, and the south Tank 5E to finish it off. Move the north Tank 1E 1S. Build an Artillery in the north base.

Black Hole:

Mechs will move forward and capture. Anti-Air will move south and destroy the Infantry. Recon will move north.

» Day 4

Orange Star:

Move the Mech 2E and attack the enemy Recon. Move the south Tank 1N 1E and attack the Anti-Air. Move Artillery 1N 4E, and the remaining Tank 1N. Build a Tank in the north base.

Black Hole:

One Mech will finish capturing a city, the other one will move down and attack your Mech. Md Tank will move and attack the Tank in the city, and the AA & Recon will retreat.

» Day 5

Orange Star:

Move south Tank 3N 1E and destroy the Anti-Air. Attack Md Tank with Artillery. Move Mech 2W and the healthy Tank 1N 5E. Deploy a Recon in the north base and an Infantry in the south base. Koal's SCOP should not be charged yet.

Black Hole:

Healthy Mech will destroy the damaged Tank, and the damaged one will begin to capture a city. Md Tank will move forward and attack the healthy Tank.

» Day 6

Orange Star:

Attack and destroy the Md Tank with your Artillery. Move the healthy Tank 4N and attack the damaged Mech. Move the Recon 1N 6E and attack the Mech. Move the damaged Tank 2E 1N and kill the Mech. Move the Mech 1E 1S. Move the Infantry 1N 1E and capture the city. Create a Tank in the north base.

Black Hole:

Koal will use Trail of Woe, his SCOP, in the beginning of his turn. However, he will do nothing...

» Day 7

Orange Star:

Finish capturing the city. Move the healthier (8~9HP) Tank 1N 2E and attack the Recon. Move the Recon 6E 1N and attack the Artillery. Move the other damaged Tank 3E and attack the Artillery again. Move the remaining Tank 1N 5E, and the Artillery 4E. Build a Recon in the north Base.

Black Hole:

Mech will keep capturing the city, and nothing else.

» Day 8

Orange Star:

Use the CO Power (or the SCOP, if you can). Move the most damaged Tank 1S and destroy the Recon. Destroy the enemy Artillery with your own. Use any unit to kill the Mech, and win.

Victory!
Speed: 100pts [8 days]
Power: 100pts [2 of 7 units destroyed in a single day]
Technique: 100pts [2 of 10 units lost]

300pts S-rank!
