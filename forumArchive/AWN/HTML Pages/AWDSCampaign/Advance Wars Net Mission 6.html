Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Advance Wars DS - Normal Campaign

Mission 06: The Ocean Blue

Difficulty: *
Days: 8
Armies: OS (Colin) vs BH (Lash)
Walkthrough: 5-days 300pts S-rank
OS Units [10]: Infantry, Tank, APC, Artillery, Rocket, B-Copter, Lander, Cruiser, Sub, Battleship
BH Units [7]: Infantry, Tank, Rocket, Lander, Cruiser, Sub, Battleship
OS Buildings [3]: City (2), HQ
BH Buildings [1]: HQ
Neutral [2]: City (2)

This is, again, a very easy map. Sasha will explain what the sea units do. All you have to do is attack the enemy units; some of them are even in your range in the first turn.

Attack the Cruiser and the Rocket; don't attack the Sub in the first turn or it will be destroyed by the Battleship. In the first turn, move the Sub forward and dive it, so it can attack the Battleship safely in the next turn. The only units worth loading in the Lander are the Tank and Infantry, but if you play fast, you won't even need them. You should have no problems with the rest of the mission.

» Day 1

Orange Star:

Attack the Rocket with your Battleship, and the Cruiser with your Rocket. Move the Sub 5E and dive. Move the B-Copter 3S 3E and attack the Tank. Move the Cruiser 3E. Load the Tank and the Infantry into your Lander, and move it 2E 3S 1E.

Black Hole:

Sub will move west and dive. The Tank will retreat to the HQ, and the sea units will move forward (south).

» Day 2

Orange Star:

Move the Sub 1E 1N and attack the Battleship. Move the Rocket 1E 1S. Move the Cruiser 1N and attack the enemy Sub. Move the Battleship 4E, and the Lander 3S 3E. Attack and destroy the Rocket with your B-Copter.

Black Hole:

Lash's Battleship and Sub will attack your own B-Ship. The Cruiser will attack your Sub, and the Infantry will attack your B-Copter.

» Day 3

Orange Star:

Move the Cruiser 1S and destroy the Sub. Attack and Destroy the Cruiser with your Rocket. Attack the Battleship with your Sub, the Tank with your Battleship, and the Infantry with your B-Copter. Move the Lander 1S 2E and drop the Infantry to the right and Tank to the south of it.

Black Hole:

Lash will use her SCOP. Battleship will attack the Cruiser, and her Tank will attack your Tank. Infantry and Lander will retreat.

» Day 4

Orange Star:

Move your Sub 4N 1E. Move the Cruiser 1N 3E and attack the Battleship (it may survive...). Destroy the Tank with your B-Copter, and the Infantry with your Tank. Move the Battleship 3N and the Rocket 2N. Move the Infantry 3E and begin capturing the HQ. Move the Lander 3N 1E.

Black Hole:

If the Battleship is alive, it will attack your Infantry, but no damage should be done. The Lander will retreat north.

» Day 5

Orange Star:

If you want, use the SCOP and attack the Lander with your Sub and the Battleship (if alive) with any unit. Capture the HQ to win.
