Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Advance Wars DS - Normal Campaign

Mission 01: Jake's Trial

Difficulty: *
Days: 7
Armies: OS (Jake) vs BM (Rachel)
Walkthrough: 3-days 300pts S-rank


OS Units [3]: Infantry, Mech, Tank
BM Units [3]: Infantry (2), Tank

OS Buildings [1]: City
BM Buildings [1]: City
Neutral [3]: City (3)

The first missions of AWDS are a tutorial, focused on teaching new players the game mechanics. If you have played an Advance Wars game before you will have no problems in beating them and even S-ranking them; if you are new to the series, Rachel will guide you through. In this first map, all you have to do is move your units to the tiles adjacent to enemy units, attack and destroy them. With the same number of troops as the enemy but going first and having them in your attack range, it's pretty difficult to lose. You will be fighting Blue Moon in this mission exceptionally.

Rachel will teach you about the map screen, the controls and the units. Tap the stylus over your orange (red?) colored units to select them, or move the cursor to them and press the A button. The rest will be explained by Rachel. Attack the enemy Tank with your Mech and Tank first - in the next turns, destroy the two Infantries.

» Day 1

Orange Star:

Move the Tank 2N 3E and attack the enemy Tank. Move the Mech 2E, attack and destroy the Tank. Then move the Infantry 1N 2E and begin capturing the neutral city.

Blue Moon:

Rachel will move her units forward. One of the infantries will begin to capture a neutral city.

» Day 2

Orange Star:

Move the Tank 1N and attack the Infantry. Jake's troops get an attack boost on plains. Move the Mech 1E 1N and attack the other Infantry. Now you'll be ignoring Rachel's advice, but it will be faster this way... Move the Infantry 3E and kill the enemy Infantry.

Blue Moon:

The surviving soldier will do nothing but continue to capture the city.

» Day 3

Orange Star:

Move any unit to an adjacent tile to the remaining enemy unit, attack it, destroy it and... win!

Victory!
Speed: 100pts 3 days
Power: 100pts 1 of 3 units destroyed in a single day
Technique: 100pts 0 of 3 units lost

300pts S-rank!
