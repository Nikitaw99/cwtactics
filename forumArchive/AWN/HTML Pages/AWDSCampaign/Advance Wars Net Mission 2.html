Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Advance Wars DS - Normal Campaign

Mission 02: The New Black

Difficulty: *
Days: 8
Armies: OS (Jake) vs BH (Jugger)
Walkthrough: 4-days 300pts S-rank



OS Units [5]: Infantry, APC, Tank, Artillery (2)
BH Units [3]: Tank (3)

OS Buildings [4]: City (3), HQ
BH Buildings: -
Neutral: -

Another tutorial mission. If you don't know yet, you will now learn to use indirect-combat units and how to repair damaged units. The general strategy here is to attack the enemy Tanks with your artillery units. Use your Tank's backup if needed.

In the first turns, attack and destroy the Tank units, one by one. Don't let them get out of both Artilleries' firing range - move the APC to the northeast and block the enemy path. You won't even have to move your Tank and your Infantry.

» Day 1

Orange Star:

Attack the only Tank in range with your eastern Artillery. Move the remaining Artillery 1E. Move the APC 4N 2E. Order your Infantry to wait; Rachel will not let you end your turn otherwise.

Black Hole:

Jugger will move his troops forward.

» Day 2

Orange Star:

Order both Artilleries to attack the tanks in range. One of them will be destroyed. Move the APC 2N 3E.

Black Hole:

Troops will be moved forward.

» Day 3

Orange Star:

Use the left Artillery to destroy the damaged Tank. Attack the healthy Tank with the other one. Move the APC 1S 1E to block the surviving Tank's path.

Black Hole:

The Tank will attack your APC.

» Day 4

Orange Star:

Order either Artillery to attack and destroy the enemy Tank.

Victory!
Speed: 100pts 4 days
Power: 100pts 1 of 3 units destroyed in a single day
Technique: 100pts 0 of 5 units lost

300pts S-rank!
