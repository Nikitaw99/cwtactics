Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Medal Guide

As you play through any mode of AW:DS, everything you do is being recorded. The number of steps you take, the number of direct attacks, the number of routs, how many Black Cannons you've destroyed, etc. These stats are all kept in the History section, and as you get to certain levels of those stats, the game awards you with medals. As you get more medals, you get a better overall rank. The chart here will help you figure out what you need to get more medals.

Ranks
Iron: 10 medals
Steel: 50 medals
Bronze: 100 medals
Silver: 150 medals
?: ? medals

Notes about this guide:

  • In the "All" section, you'll find Units Selected and Spaces Moved. In the game, these are "Number of Steps Taken" and "Units Moved", respectively. We believe they flipped these, so we labeled them as they should be.
  • You'll probably notice a lot of boxes have just "???" in them. That's because we haven't figured out what goes there yet. This is a work in progress, people! If you have info about what does go in a specific box, please tell us on the forums! (To be specific, this topic.)
