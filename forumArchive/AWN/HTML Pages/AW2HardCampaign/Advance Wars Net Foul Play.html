Advance Wars 2: Black Hole Rising

HC Foul Play

Sensei vs. Adder
Red Difficulty: *******

General Strategy: Don't get fooled by the two-front war...there is only one. You want your air force to reach your home base ASAP to save it, which means your entire air force except for a bomber should be blowing through the seems. However, since you can't send everything at once, you can destroy some southern units while you wait. In the north, don't even try defending at the forests. It won't work. Instead, use artillery and medium tanks that you build to attack the seems...maybe even an infantry. In any case, after the arrival of the bombers it's really easy, and your Super CO Power gives you an edge in the northeast as you take over a few areas with those mechs. Use those bombers to take out the minicannons after those units are cleared. Do so in 11 days.

End:

Victory! 100 Speed + 100 Power + 100 Technique = 300 S-Rank
