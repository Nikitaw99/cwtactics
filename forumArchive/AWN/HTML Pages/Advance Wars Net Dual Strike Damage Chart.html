Advance Wars: Dual Strike

Damage Chart

AW:DS Damage Chart

Here you can find a list of the base damage units do in Advance Wars DS. They are based in an Andy vs Andy matchup, with 100% attack and defense 10HP units as default, and in 0-star terrains. There are some differences from AW2, such as the Cruisers' ability to attack any sea units. The defensive value of sea units in general (the Cruiser's in special) has also increased, as well as the firepower of Cruisers. Copters now have slight more defense against anti-aerial units as well. And there are, of course, many new units. Keep in mind that Black Hole structures/weapons like the Pipeline and Cannons have the same defensive value as a Md Tank, so they are not in the list.
