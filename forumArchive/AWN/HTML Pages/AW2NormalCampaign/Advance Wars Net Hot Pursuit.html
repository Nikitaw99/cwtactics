Advance Wars 2: Black Hole Rising

Hot Pursuit

OS CO (Andy) + BM CO (Grit) + GE CO (Jess) vs. Sturm
Difficulty: ******

"Attack the Black Hole citadel!" Here is the first mission you fight Sturm in. Don't spell Strum, please...unless you think it's funny, haha. Sturm is informed by his lieutenant Allied force movements into Black Hole itself. Flak and Adder argue over taking command, but Sturm insists on taking command himself. "You wretches report to the missile platform and assist Lash." They leave, and Sturm is very disappointed with their losses. "When this is finished, I will make...adjustments."

Max tells Sami that this is Sturm's citadel, and Sami says that they pillaged everything from allied countries and have them here. Jess agrees to that, and Andy argues to get back their money here. Nell thinks it got moved. Eagle and Olaf ask why, and Grit thinks the same as Nell. Kanbei comes and asks for a general assault to take out the cannons. Sonja says you must take out the 3 Black Cannons. They will run if you take them out. Sensei agrees, and then they'd find out if that was Sturm's HQ. "That's brilliant, Commander Sonja!" - Colin. Indirect fire is useful here, and using decoys, as well as what COs you use.

I suggest a combo of Andy, Grit, and Jess. OS gets 12 properties, while BM and GE get 10. YC doesn't seem to have an army.

  • Andy Day 1: Build two infantry.
  • Grit Day 1: Build two infantry.
  • Jess Day 1: Build two infantry.
  • Sturm Day 1: Both laser cannons fire and miss. Move down sea units in the west and east.
  • Andy Day 2: Move both infantry 3N. Build two infantry in western base, rocket in eastern base.
  • Grit Day 2: Move western infantry 3E. Move eastern infantry 2E 1N. Build a rocket from eastern base and infantry from western base.
  • Jess Day 2: Move both infantry 1S 2W. Build a rocket from western base, infantry from eastern base.
  • Sturm Day 2: Cannon blasts miss. Move down sea units.
  • Andy Day 3: Move northwest infantry 1N 2W, northeast infantry 1N 2E. Move rocket 4N. Move other infantry 3N. Build a rocket from western base, infantry from eastern base.
  • Grit Day 3: Move your rocket 3E 1N. Move northeast infantry 3N to start taking base. Move eastern infantry 1NE, western infantry 3E. Build no more.
  • Jess Day 3: Move your rocket 1S 4W. Move western infantry 2W 1N. Move central infantry 2W. Move eastern infantry 1S 2W.
  • Sturm Day 3: Move down naval forces.
  • Andy Day 4: Move northwest infantry 1W to start capturing that base. Move the northeast infantry 1E to start capturing that base. Use top rocket to indirectly attack the minicannon. Move western rocket 4N. Move now northwest infantry 1N 2W. Move southeast infantry 3N.
  • Grit Day 4: Finish capturing base. Move rocket 3N. Move other two infantry 2N. Build a rocket from western base.
  • Jess Day 4: Move rocket 3N. Move infantry in the west 3N to take base. Move eastern infantry 3W. Move central infantry 1W 2N. Build a rocket from the eastern base.
  • Sturm Day 4: Cannon blast nothing. Move down navies. Attack Grit's eastern rocket with his battleship.
  • Andy Day 5: Use eastern rocket to take out minicannon. Finish taking both bases. Move central infantry 2E 1N. Move left rocket 1E. Build a medium tank from left base.
  • Grit Day 5: Use both rockets to weaken sub to 1HP. Move northeast bottom two infantry.
  • Jess Day 5: Finish taking base. Attack cruiser with eastern rocket. Build a T-Copter and an infantry. Move western rocket 2N, infantry 2N 1W.
  • Sturm Day 5: Laser attack nothing. Take out Grit's 1HP rocket with battleship. Move cruisers south (one to attack T-Copter).
  • Andy Day 6: With western rocket, attack minicannon. Move infantry on NE base 1E. Move rocket onto NE base. Move medium tank 4N 1W. Make a medium tank from SE base.
  • Grit Day 6: Attack cruiser with rocket. Move infantry on northern base 1S. Build a rocket from that base.
  • Jess Day 6: Take out cruiser with eastern rocket. Move infantry into T-Copter. Move T-Copter 4N 1W and drop infantry north onto the port (out of battleship range). Move infantry on northern base 1W. Build a rocket from that base.
  • Sturm Day 6: Cannons fire and miss. Move weakened sub up and rise in the west. Dive sub in the east. Move battleship 1E in the west, move 5HP cruiser north off BM port in the west.
  • Andy Day 7: Use both rockets to attack both minicannons, taking out minicannon in the west. Move eastern rocket onto base. Move eastern medium tank 4N 1E. Build a medium tank from the western base.
  • Grit Day 7: Use both rockets to blast the battleship into the ocean waters.
  • Jess Day 7: Move eastern rocket 1S 4W.
  • Sturm Day 7: Lash's laser cannons manage to hit nothing and they themselves do not get hurt. Dive western 1HP sub, rise eastern sub.
  • Andy Day 8: Take out minicannon with eastern rocket. Move infantry 5 under the minicannon 1S. Move western rocket 3W. Move medium tank on western base 1E 4N. Make medium tank on eastern base.
  • Grit Day 8: Move northeast rocket 1N. Destroy cruiser with western rocket. Build medium tank from northern base.
  • Jess Day 8: Start capturing port next to battleship. Move SE rocket 1W 2N. Build a bomber.
  • Sturm Day 8: Laser cannon fires at nothing. "Such power...I regret crushing it." Super CO Power Meteor Strike smashes huge damage on Jess' three rockets and infantry. Rise western sub. Dive sub in the east. Move eastern battleship north.
  • Andy Day 9: Blast western minicannon with western rocket. Move medium tank on eastern base 1W 4N. Build medium tank from western base.
  • Grit Day 9: Kill sub with western rocket. Build a battleship.
  • Jess Day 9: Finish taking port. Move bomber 5N. Move 4HP rocket 3S. Move bottom 2HP rocket 1SW. Move top 2HP rocket 1S.
  • Sturm Day 9: Stupid laser cannons fire at nothing. Attack infantry that took port with battleship. Rise sub in the east.
  • Andy Day 10: Seal Sturm's minicannon fate by taking it out with your western rocket. Move medium tank on western base 4N. Move infantry on northeast base 2E 1S and build an APC.
  • Grit Day 10: Move battleship 4N 1W. Move western rocket 4E 1N.
  • Jess Day 10: Bomb submarine below your bomber. Move 2HP infantry 1S into transport copter 6S, and drop east.
  • Sturm Day 10: Laser cannons hit nothing. Dive sub.
  • Andy Day 11: Move western three medium tanks 5N. The eastern of the three should start breaching a pipeline. Move eastern medium tank 5N and the one west of it 4N. Move eastern rocket 4N and western rocket 1W 3N. Move infantry to capture neutral cities. Move APC 6E. Build a medium tank from NE base.
  • Grit Day 11: Move battleship 4N. Move medium tank 5N. Move northern rocket 4N. Move southern rocket 3N onto base. Move infantry towards NW penninsula.
  • Jess Day 11: Move bomber 1N to start bombing the battleship. Move all rockets north, preferably along the road to go faster. Move infantry towards NE penninsula. Build a rocket from northern base.
  • Sturm Day 11: Attack three of Andy's medium tanks with Black Cannons. Laser cannons hit nothing. Rise sub.
  • Andy Day 12: Attack laser cannon in the east with eastern rocket. From NE base medium tank, move it 5N to attack the pipeline. Move 2nd to west medium tank that has full HP 4E to attack the pipeline. Use the western rocket to attack pipeline. Move western 5HP medium tank 1E. Move western full HP medium tank 2E to finish off pipeline. Move last two 5HP medium tanks through pipeline. Continue taking middle cities. Build medium tank from western base.
  • Grit Day 12: Move battleship 4E 1N. Attack western laser cannon with rocket. Move Grit's medium tank 4E to take out the pipeline. Start taking NW penninsula base, and move other infantry towards other props there. Move back rocket 3N. Build another battleship.
  • Jess Day 12: Sink the battleship with your bomber, attacking up. Use front rocket to weaken eastern laser cannon to 8HP. Move all other rockets 3N except the SW one that moves 2N. Move infantry to NE penninsula.
  • Sturm Day 12: Black Cannon blasts three of Andy's other medium tanks. Laser cannons hit nothing as always. Dive sub.
  • Andy Day 13: With eastern rocket, take out the farthest to the west pipeline seem you can in one hit. Attack easternmost seem with 5HP medium tank from bottom, then send another 5HP medium tank to the north of it and take it out. Move western rocket 2N. Move all other medium tanks eastward. Continue taking cities. The remainder of the infantry should move north with the APC. Move western base medium tank 4N 1E. Build medium tank from eastern base.
  • Grit Day 13: Use bottom rocket to wipe out laser cannon. Use top rocket to attack medium tank. Use 5HP battleship to attack rocket. Move medium tank 2N. Move back medium tank 4N 1W. Continue taking props from NW penninsula. Build a rocket from northern base.
  • Jess Day 13: Use 2nd to top rocket (just has enough range to take out laser cannon) to do so. Move other rockets as far north as you can. Move bomber 3W. Build a medium tank from northern base.
  • Sturm Day 13: Black cannon blast one Andy medium tank, one Andy rocket, and one Grit medium tank. Take out 5HP medium tank that blew up pipeline with rocket (east). Rise sub. Move down medium tanks and rockets on both sides (outer pipeline region).
  • Andy Day 14: Use western rocket to eliminate 7HP rocket. Attack Sturm's 5HP medium tank with your 5HP medium tank. In the east, attack ONE medium tank from rocket and two sides with 5HP medium tanks. Attack pipeline for power (but don't destroy any or factory will start doing stuff). Finish taking cities, move up eastern medium tank 5N. Build medium tank from NW base. Move up infantry and APC.
  • Grit Day 14: Super Snipe! Use 5HP battleship to attack rocket in inner pipeline. Use both rockets to wipe out full HP medium tank. Use 5HP medium tank and move it west to weaken 3HP medium tank to 1HP. Continue taking cities on NW penninsula. Move bottom battleship 4N. Move bottom rocket 3N 1E. Build a rocket from northern base.
  • Jess Day 14: Use front two rockets to wipe out 4HP medium tank and weaken rocket. Move bomber 3E to take out sub. Continue taking cities on NE penninsula. Move up other two rockets. Move medium tank NW. Build another medium tank.
  • Sturm Day 14: Black Cannon blast three medium tanks (two Andy, one Jess). Meteor Strike on Jess' four weakened rockets and weakened medium tank (good, cause Grit can't take too much). Take out four OS medium tanks and one GE rocket with rockets and medium tanks.
  • Andy Day 15: Move medium tank on western base 1E 4N. Move western rocket 1NE. Take out 1HP medium tank in the west with 5HP medium tank. In the east, attack medium tank with rocket, attack rocket with 5HP medium tank, and attack 8HP medium tank with other medium tank. Hyper Upgrade. Move infantry northeast with extra step, as well as APC east. Build medium tank from NE base.
  • Grit Day 15: Move northern battleship (5HP) 3N 1W. Move other battleship 4E 1N. Use northern rocket and weakened medium tank to weaken missile. Move up other rockets NORTH, one onto the shoal. Continue taking penninsula cities.
  • Jess Day 15: Use messed up rockets to attack whatever you can. Do the same with GE medium tank. Continue taking penninsula cities. Move bomber west but out of cannon/missile range. Move medium tank northwest. Build another medium tank. Move infantry/t-copter west.
  • Sturm Day 15: Black Cannon blasts two of Andy's medium tanks and one of Jess' full medium tanks. Attack medium tanks with rockets, take out Jess' 1HP rocket, attack infantry with 8HP medium tank of Andy's.
  • Andy Day 16: Fuel eastern rocket with APC. Move that rocket 1NW. Attack western black cannon with western rocket 55% to 44HP. Move all other units up. Make medium tank from NW and NE bases. Take out eastern rocket with 4HP medium tank. Attack pipeline if you feel bored (for power) but don't kill any.
  • Grit Day 16: Attack 6HP rocket to 2HP with 5HP battleship. Move other battleship 3N INTO ROCKET RANGE. Use 5HP rocket to finish off missile. Move 1HP medium tank to 2 west of 2HP rocket. Move rockets NE. Finish taking penninsula cities and start moving east. Build an APC in northern base and rocket from new NW base.
  • Jess Day 16: Move bomber west to take damage next turn. Finish taking penninsula props and head northwest with all units except a 2HP rocket that should attack a full health missile. Build a medium tank from northern base.
  • Sturm Day 16: Black Cannon blasts BM rocket on NW base, bomber and medium tanks of GE. 2HP rocket deals 1 damage to battleship, rocket kills GE full health infantry in one hit, and finishes off 2HP infantry with 8HP medium tank.
  • Andy Day 17: Use western rocket to take out western Black Cannon! Use eastern rocket to attack 8HP medium tank to 4. Move up full health medium tank NW to take it out. Attack missile in the east with 4HP medium tank. Move up medium tanks and APC. Build a rocket from NW base.
  • Grit Day 17: Use 5HP battleship to take out 2HP rocket. Attack missile with other battleship. Fuel rockets with APC, move rockets east. Build rocket from NE base.
  • Jess Day 17: Attack missile, move units up.
  • Sturm Day 17: Blast Andy's medium tank, Jess' medium tank. Finish 1HP medium tank of Jess with rocket.
  • Andy Day 18: Keep pulling rockets north. Finish off missile. Move up medium tanks. Fuel rocket. Move everything up, make rocket from NE base.
  • Grit Day 18: Take out missile and weaken medium tank with battleships. Move rockets east/northeast, as with infantry.
  • Jess Day 18: Move units northwest. Do not use Override yet. Build a medium tank.
  • Sturm Day 18: Get furious and attack two Andy medium tanks with Black Cannons.
  • Andy Day 19: Move western rocket 2E, eastern rocket 2N. The western rocket should be ready to fire on the central Black Cannon next two turns. Move other medium tanks/rockets up. Build a Neotank from eastern base.
  • Grit Day 19: Move up rockets to cannons even if they can fire on something. Build a medium tank.
  • Jess Day 19: Overdrive to bring up units fast and without terrain penalty. Move everything up! Build a Neotank from NW base.
  • Sturm Day 19: Black Cannon blasts OS rocket, GE medium tank. Blow up bomber and medium tank.
  • Andy Day 20: Attack central Black Cannon with western rocket. Move up units and attack what you can. Build a Neotank from eastern base.
  • Grit Day 20: Move rockets east. Move medium tank northeast. Build a Neotank. Attack Neotank with battleship.
  • Jess Day 20: Attack what you can and move up Neotank. Build rocket.
  • Sturm Day 20: Black Cannon attacks OS and GE Neotanks. Other tanks attack as well and take out some units.
  • Andy Day 21: Hyper Repair! Use western rocket to take out central Black Cannon. Use other units to blast a Neotank, and weaken another. Build a rocket from NE base.
  • Grit Day 21: Snipe Attack! Use rockets to attack two Neotanks and one medium tank. Move up other units.
  • Jess Day 21: Move up units and take out a medium tank. Move up units.
  • Sturm Day 21: Black Cannon blasts OS Neotank. Meteor Strike on OS+BM rockets (mostly BM). Take out 4 medium tanks.
  • Andy Day 22: Attack as much enemy units as possible with rockets first, then medium tanks. Take out one if possible and make a Neotank in eastern base.
  • Grit Day 22: Same as Andy, and move rockets east.
  • Jess Day 22: Move up units and make Neotank.
  • Sturm Day 22: Attack BM Neotank with Black Cannon. Wipe out three units.
  • Andy Day 23: Attack with rockets first. Hyper Repair. Take out Neotank and weaken medium tank. Move up another Neotank. Make another one.
  • Grit Day 23: Wipe out all but a 5HP medium tank, a rocket, and a missile. Move units up.
  • Jess Day 23: Weaken medium tank to 1HP. Move up units.
  • Sturm Day 23: Blast GE medium tank, weaken GE infantry.
  • Andy Day 24: Take out last medium tank, move strongest Neotank at 7HP to attack eastern Black Cannon. Move units up.
  • Grit Day 24: Move up rockets.
  • Jess Day 24: End.
  • Sturm Day 24: Black Cannon blast back OS Neotank.
  • Andy Day 25: Finish off Black Cannon with front Neotank.

Sturm is informed his Black Cannons have fallen, and sends an infantry that is captured to tell of Sturm's real army to follow the trail to Sturm's missile platform and be trapped, and launch missiles at Cosmo Land and other continents. Nell got the intel through their own intel-gathering team as well as through the soldier. Grit suspects it is a trap. Sonja agrees. Allied commanders agree to attack with morale still high and time still enough. "I'll win next time, too!" - Andy.

Victory! 94 Speed + 34 Power + 69 Technique = 197 Pts. = C-Rank

OK, here we enter the last stage of the war, disarming and defeating Sturm. Move to the last mission, a ******* one.
