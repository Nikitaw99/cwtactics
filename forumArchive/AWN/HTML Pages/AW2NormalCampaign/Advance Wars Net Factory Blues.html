Advance Wars 2: Black Hole Rising

Factory Blues

Grit and Max vs. Lash
Difficulty: ****

Flak, Lash, and Adder talk about how to save the remainder of their holdings in Blue Moon, the factory. Olaf and Grit say they're ready for this challenge, when Nell pops up under Grit's request. Grit's help turns out to really be useful when an OS-BM alliance is helpful in defeating BH. Choose BM Grit and OS Max. Grit has the bases while Max has the predeployed units. You'll want Max to get the two bases.

  • Grit Day 1: Build three infantry.
  • Max Day 1: Move bottom tank 6E. Move top tank 5E 1N. Move artillery 4E 1N. Move A-Air 5E 1N. Move mech 2E. Move infantry into APC. Move APC 5E 1N drop east. Move recon 5E 1N.
  • Lash Day 1: She builds two infantry and has a mech out of the factory.
  • Grit Day 2: Capture the three CITIES next to you. Do not capture ANY base, give those to Max. Make three infantry.
  • Max Day 2: Move mech 2E to begin capturing base. Move infantry 3E. Move tank 5N. Move APC 3W. Move the artillery 2E 3N. Move recon 1E 3N. Move other tank and A-Air 1E 5N.
  • Lash Day 2: 3 recons out of factories that move south. Make one APC.
  • Grit Day 3: Finish capturing three cities. Move left and right infantries 3N and the middle one 2N 1E. Build nothing.
  • Max Day 3: Finish capturing left base with mech. Move infantry 1S to start capturing that base. Don't move APC, leave it there. Move the back tank 1E 4N to kill the recon there. Move the other tank 5N. Move artillery, A-Air and recon 4N.
  • Lash Day 3: Start capturing airport, attack tank with mech, build a tank.
  • Grit Day 4: Move up infantry and capture cities. Create an artillery from northern base.
  • Max Day 4: Finish capturing right base, use APC to move mech to western two cities so Max can have them. Build an infantry from western base. Move A-Air 2N 1W to finish off the mech. Move non-injured tank 1E 3N and injure that recon. Move 4HP tank 1E 2N to kill that recon. Move recon 1W 3N and artillery 3N.
  • Lash Day 4: Finish capturing a city, attack artillery with recon, move up tank, make infantry and A-Air, factory-produce an artillery.
  • Grit Day 5: Move artillery 1W 4N. Capture cities in north and east (west is for Max). Prepare to fire a missile next day. Create three infantry.
  • Max Day 5: Begin capturing city with mech, add an infantry to get the other one. Move the other one 3N from eastern base. Deploy two infantry. Attack infantry with artillery. Move A-Air 1N to attack recon. Attack artillery with tank. Finish infantry with recon. Move 4HP tank 2W to kill recon. Move APC in route for western base infantry to go up quickly.
  • Lash Day 5: Artillery injures 4HP tank, A-Air from factory attacks BM infantry, tank kills 1HP tank, mech kills artillery.
  • Grit Day 6: Attack A-Air with your artillery to deal it 8 damage. Finish capturing cities. Launch a missile at the tank, infantry, and mech. Fire a second one at a tank and infantry. Finish off the A-Air with an infantry. Make a Neotank from top base. Move infantry north.
  • Max Day 6: Continue taking cities and moving north. Create two infantry. Move your tank 1N 3W to attack the tank, attack east with the A-Air to take out the mech, and move recon 2N 1E to finish off the artillery.
  • Lash Day 6: Move down units, finish off 2HP infantry on city with infantry. Build an artillery and infantry.
  • Grit Day 7: Use artillery to blast infantry, then use another infantry to kill it. Send a missile on 2 tanks and 2 infantry in the west of the factory. Move Neotank and infantry north.
  • Max Day 7: Max Force. Finish capturing city, and move all infantry/mechs north except for the one near the APC, which should ride it to the western airport. Move tank 1NW to take out the 6HP tank, then send A-Air 3N to take out that tank, then move recon 2W to take out the infantry. Create two infantry.
  • Lash Day 7: A-Air moves south to weaken Max's infantry, infantry moves down to attack Grit's infantry, and other units move down. An infantry and tank is built.
  • Grit Day 8: Move Neotank 4N. Take out BH 8HP infantry with a full health one and your 5HP one. Move everything north, and have a blue infantry near both airports to capture their cities (airport on right side). Remember your artillery has to be moved from your base up as well. Build a rocket.
  • Max Day 8: Capture airport in the northwest, and move all other infantry and mechs north. Build two infantry. Move APC east. Move A-Air 2N to take out that infantry, then move tank 4N to attack the infantry on the base. Move recon 2E.
  • Lash Day 8: A Neotank pops out from the factory and destroys your BM infantry. An A-Air kills your infantry. Another infantry that pops out from factory takes out your 1HP OS infantry. An infantry and tank is built.
  • Grit Day 9: Attack the A-Air with your artillery, and move infantry to form with Max's forces a solid wall so that your Neotank won't be attacked first. Kill the A-Air with your Neotank. Move everything up and capture cities on western and eastern parts of the map!
  • Max Day 9: Capture whatever properties are close to you, but move every infantry/mech north 2/3. Finish off the 6HP infantry with your front one. Build nothing as well. Move your A-Air 1S to finish off the 6HP infantry, and attack the infantry where your tank is with your tank.
  • Lash Day 9: Prime Tactics. Her tanks and artillery move up, but she does no actual damage.
  • Grit Day 10: Move two artillery in range of the enemy Neotank. From the east, attack hers with your Neotank. Create another Neotank from top base. With infantry, block the factory so she can't get any free units next turn. Finish capturing city with infantry in east.
  • Max Day 10: Capture a city or two and move infantry up. Move A-Air 2S 1E to take out the 6HP infantry, and move your APC 3N 1E. Drop the infantry to the north. Don't move your tank and attack from the west at the infantry. Move other infantry in to capture BH city.
  • Lash Day 10: Tank attacks BM infantry. Tank attacks OS infantry. Mech finishes OS infantry. BH infantry attacks OS infantry in west. Artillery moves west.
  • Grit Day 11: Snipe Attack. Both artillery to finish Neotank. Move Neotank 2NW to attack infantry. Move up infantry towards NW base. Move up Neotank and rocket.
  • Max Day 11: Start moving all units east except for the infantry and tank near western Lash base. Finish capturing city. Attack artillery with infantry. Attack infantry with infantry and infantry on base with tank.
  • Lash Day 11: Lash is doomed. She joins her 1+6=7 infantry on her west base, and in the east, kills Grit's infantry with the artillery. She also takes out the infantry on the eastern airport with a tank, but that's ok...you lured away one tank. A tank attacks an artillery, and she builds a mech.
  • Grit Day 12: Use front Neotank to deal damage to western base infantry. Capture cities with infantry. Move up 2nd Neotank. Use back rocket and artillery to take out front tank, then use your artillery to hit hers. Create another Neotank.
  • Max Day 12: Finish off infantry on western base with tank, then use 6HP infantry to start capturing the base. Move A-Air east to take out the artillery. Move red units to block factory and eastward to advance towards the right base. Create a Neotank from eastern base.
  • Lash Day 12: Move A-Air to kill Max's infantry. Make a mech. Lash currently only has 3 Mechs, 1 APC, 1 Tank, and 1 A-Air left.
  • Grit Day 13: Move Neotank, artillery, rockets, and infantry towards eastern base. Capture the two cities west of the factory, and use the 4HP NeoTank to attack the pipeline seam and deal it 24 damage. Move your back Neotank all the way north.
  • Max Day 13: Continue capturing western base, bring tank south. Attack A-Air with A-Air and send waves of infantry at Black Hole lines. Move Neotank 5N.
  • Lash Day 13: Terrain Tactics. An OS infantry is eliminated by her superior terrain bonuses and she builds a rocket. Little units, but great powerful units, eh?
  • Grit Day 14: Continue blowing up the pipeline seam with the first Neotank. Attack the A-Air with your artillery, attack the mech on the mountain with your second Neotank, and move up your artillery. Move infantry to block factory, and towards the eastern base. Also bring up third Neotank and rocket. Build a 4th Neotank for the heck of it.
  • Max Day 14: Finish off A-Air with A-Air. Move every unit to the eastern front as close to the eastern base as possible. Continue taking western base.
  • Lash Day 14: BH taking airport. Rocket takes out 6HP infantry that was taking western base.
  • Grit Day 15: Attack rocket with artillery. Continue attacking pipeline seam. Move 3rd Neotank to western front. Move everything to eastern front. Move 4th Neotank up. Build a 5th one.
  • Max Day 15: Send up infantry to take out his 2HP one. Destroy the rocket with your recon. Move your A-air to the north of the mech and attack it. Move everything else towards eastern base except for Neotank which should move east. Make a second one from eastern base of yours.
  • Lash Day 15: Medium tank comes out of factory and kills Grit's rocket. Builds an infantry from western base and NOTHING from eastern.
  • Grit Day 16: Move up Neotank on western front to attack artillery. Continue to attack pipeline seem to 3HP. Take out mech on eastern front. Move rest of units east. Move bottom Neotanks towards BH tank.
  • Max Day 16: Start taking eastern base. Use A-Air to destroy the pipeline seem. You win! With this victory, Olaf has finally saved Blue Moon from Black Hole conquest and destruction. Olaf praises both Grit and Colin for being great COs. The Orange Star COs agree that the war has come to an end in Orange Star and Blue Moon, but there was more to be done.

Victory! 98 Speed + 30 Power + 85 Technique = 213 Pts. = B Rank

Your victory brings you final victory in Blue Moon! Now it's off to clear Yellow Comet from enemy intruders!
