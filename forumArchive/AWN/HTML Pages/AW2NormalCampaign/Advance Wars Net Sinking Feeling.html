Advance Wars 2: Black Hole Rising

Sinking Feeling

Jess vs. Lash
Difficulty: ******

Hawke wants Lash to finish repairs on the battleships within 30 days. Lash says it'll take 17 days. The southernmost city on the western island contains the lab map. Jess notes that although she has few troops, she can't lose time before the repairs are complete.

  • Jess Day 1: Move NE mech to take port. Move western infantry into APC and move APC 5S, drop south. Move infantry 3E, mech 2E. Move northern medium tank 5N, southern medium tank 1N 4E. Move western artillery 1N 4E. Move eastern artillery 5E. Move rocket 1N 4E. Move northern tank 1N 4E. Move recon 3E. Move western A-Air 4E 2N. Move southern tank 1N 4E. Move last A-Air 1NE.
  • Lash Day 1: Lash and soldier talk, and Lash declares a counterattack despite repairs going on without hurting repairs. Move down 2 infantry, mech, 4 tanks, artillery, A-Air, 2 medium tanks, and rocket. Move 2 infantry on northeast island 3W.
  • Jess Day 2: Finish capturing port. Move NE medium tank 1N 3E, and other medium tank 1N 4E. Move southern mech 2E. Move eastern infantry 1S. Move northern tank 2E, southern tank 3E. Move recon 2E. Move left artillery 2E. Move northern A-Air 1E. Move southern A-Air 1SE.
  • Lash Day 2: Move down units, start capturing NE airport.
  • Jess Day 3: Move southern mech 2N to attack tank. Use rocket to attack medium tank. Southern medium tank 2N to take out medium tank. Move unmoved medium tank 2NE to weaken tank. Move northern mech 2E to attack tank. Move top A-Air 5E 1N to take out mech to east. Move northern tank 5E 1N to attack A-Air. Move other A-Air 1N 5E to attack infantry. Build a battleship from port. Move both artillery 2E 1N. Move southern infantry 2E. Move southern tank 3N 1E to take out tank. Move northern infantry 2S. Move recon 3N 2E to take out infantry. Move APC 4E 2S.
  • Lash Day 3: Finish taking cities in south, move down tanks to attack A-Air, tank, and recon. Move down medium tank, rocket, infantry towards base.
  • Jess Day 4: Move up battleship 3N 2E. Move rocket 5E. Build a lander. Move full HP mech 2W into lander. Use southern artillery to take out 3HP A-Air. Move non-weakened tank 1S 2E to attack tank. Turbo Charge. Use weakened A-Air and weakened tanks to take out 4HP tank (from left and right without moving). Move southern medium tank 5E to attack enemy medium tank. Move northern medium tank 1N 2E to weaken tank. Move recon 2NE to try to take it out. Move injured mech 2N. Move other A-Air 2S 4E. Move top infantry 2E. Move bottom infantry 1E into APC, and move APC 3E 2N and drop west. Move northern artillery 4E 1N.
  • Lash Day 4: Prime Tactics. Retreat weakened units to rocket, finish weakened tank with weakened medium tank. Move rocket 1E.
  • Jess Day 5: Move battleship 5N. Move northern medium tank 4E 1N. Move lander 4N 2W. Kill weakened medium tank with rocket. Move both artillery 3E 2N. Move other medium tank 3E 2N. Move bottom A-Air 2N 3E to injure artillery. Move mech 1NE. Move northern A-Air 5N 1E. Move tank 1N 4E. Move APC 1E 4N. Move southern infantry 1S 2E. Move northern infantry 1E. Move recon 5E. End.
  • Lash Day 5: Finish capturing city/bases, attack artillery with rocket.
  • Jess Day 6: Overdrive. Lander 6N. Attack NE battleship with battleship. Move 9HP tank 2E 3N to take out rocket. Move up full HP medium tank 2N to take out infantry. Move recon 9N 1E. Move back 8HP medium tank 6N 1E. Move mech 2E. Attack artillery with A-Air from north without moving it. Move northern infantry 1S 3E to take it out. Move southern infantry 2E to capture city. Move full HP artillery 2N 5E. Move rocket 4N. Move 2HP artillery 5N. APC 2E 3N to block BH base. Weakened A-Air 2E 6N.
  • Lash Day 6: Start capturing second airport from NE island, move battle copter southwest. Move up 1HP tank.
  • Jess Day 7: Move 8HP medium tank 3W to attack minicannon. Continue attacking unhurt battleships with your battleship. Move lander 1N 5W and drop mech north. Move other medium tank 4N 1E. Start capturing base in southeast and finish taking city. A-Air from SE 2N 4E. Mech 2N. Use 2HP artillery to attack minicannon. Move 4HP A-Air 5N 1W. Move recon 3N 2W to take out 1HP tank. Move APC 6N. Move full health artillery 3N 1E onto BH base. Move 9HP tank 5N. Move rocket 2E 1N. Build a battleship and end.
  • Lash Day 7: Attack 8HP medium tank to 5HP. Terrain Tactics. Destroy recon with bomber. Move battle copter south.
  • Jess Day 8: Continue capturing props from southeast. Finish off minicannon with rocket. Attack bomber with A-Air that will die. Let artillery take minicannon fire that is at 2HP. Move A-Air onto BH base. Move mech up. Move everything toward battleships. On western island, move mech 1W to start capturing that city to take lab map. Move lander 6E as bait.
  • Lash Day 8: Doesn't take bait and attacks full HP medium tank with upper minicannon, moves bomber to take out artillery. Move copter down to hit tank. Build ANOTHER bomber.
  • Jess Day 9: Move up second battleship, make a 3rd, attack with 1st battleship. Move up rocket. Move NW full HP A-Air, move mech 2E, move artillery onto BH base. Finish capturing everything in southeast. Build a recon. Attack weakened battleships with tanks. Move APC 1N. Move lander 3NE. Finish capturing with western mech to get a...lab map, and Neotanks coming your way soon! 1 battleship should be down now due to joining battleships.
  • Lash Day 9: Burn A-Air with cannon. Bomb and copter down rocket, use other copter to lure to lander. Move T-Copter down.
  • Jess Day 10: Attack plane with A-Air, weaken two battleships. Prepare to use Overdrive next day. Move up 3rd battleship. Move lander NE. Capture port with western infantry. Move up recon. Move mech 2E. Move up infantry from southeast. Build A-Air. Keep attacking battleships with two medium tanks and one tank.
  • Lash Day 10: Burn APC with minicannon. Join 5+4HP battleships. Bomb down lander that lures away bomber. Bomb battleship with other bomber. Attack artillery and medium tank with battle copters.
  • Jess Day 11: Overdrive! Finish taking NW port. Move up 3rd battleship. Aim for one battleship with both battleships in front, plus both medium tanks to take it out so she only has 5 left! Move A-Air 2E to take out battle copter. Move artillery NE. Start capturing NE base with mech. Move up infantry and A-Air. Be happy that you're still on track to win!
  • Lash Day 11: Cannon blast APC to 4HP. Prime Tactics. Bomber bombs down APC. Bomber kills artillery (perfect bait for A-Air). Battle copter destroys small 3HP tank in battleship zone. Move back T-Copter, build a battle copter.
  • Jess Day 12: Move mech on western island to take final city. Build battleship from that port on the island. With both full HP battleships and two medium tanks, take out another battleship. Weaken another one with 5HP battleship. Take out bomber with A-Air, and move up other one as well. Continue taking BH base with mech. Move recon northwest. Move infantry north for bait later. Build FINAL A-Air from SE base.
  • Lash Day 12: Cannon blasts one A-Air. Bomber finishes 5HP battleship. Battle copter does 2 damage to battleship, other battle copter moves down. Move T-Copter to heal.
  • Jess Day 13: Move full HP battleship west to weaken bomber to 2HP. Move up other weakened A-Air to battleship zone, avoiding cannon. Move recon west. On western island, finish capturing city. You now have three full battleships, two that can attack. There are four battleships left to take out, and you have 5 days. Move newly built battleship 5E. Check its range. Do not kill ANY battleship in that range, but you can weaken them. With two battleships at half-HP due to your two battleships and medium tanks, they will surely join together to become three overall. Move up infantry. Continue taking BH base with mech. Move northwest final A-Air. Have nothing in cannon range.
  • Lash Day 13: Weaken battleship and medium tank from bridge, which means A-Air can attack next turn. Other battle copter takes infantry bait instead of hitting battleship. Build nothing.
  • Jess Day 14: Overdrive! Move 3rd battleship NE. Use other two battleships to wipe out the 8HP joined battleship. Move 9HP A-Air up to take out full HP battle copter. Move down 4HP A-Air to take out bomber. Use remaining medium tank to attack cannon from west. Finish off final battle copter. Now Lash has no way of winning. No chance. Finish capturing base with mech. Move up recon only to bridges out of cannon range. Build a battleship from western island port.
  • Lash Day 14: Doomed as mushrooms, she uses Prime Tactics...and can't do anything. Haha.
  • Jess Day 15: Continue smashing cannon with medium tank from the left. Pull up farthest battleship NE. Weaken a battleship to 2HP. Pull last battleship east just in case.
  • Lash Day 15: Think it's still a game, keep healing T-Copter.
  • Jess Day 16: Use 6HP battleship to down 2HP battleship. Use other two battleships in range to weaken battleship and finish off cannon. Move up final battleship.
  • Lash Day 16: Lash only has one day to finish, a BH infantry reports. Lash thinks that they are almost finished so doesn't care. Do nothing.
    • Jess Day 17: Final day. Use ANY battleship to sink her battleship. If it isn't enough, add another one, but I doubt it. Jess tells everyone to retreat after their victory because more BH forces will win. Nice going...victory! Lash no longer feels it's like a game, so it's time you take her seriously? Lash is angry.

Victory! 96 Speed + 100 Power + 79 Technique = 275 Pts. = A-Rank

Four new missions open up here. Move to the southern mission with Drake.
