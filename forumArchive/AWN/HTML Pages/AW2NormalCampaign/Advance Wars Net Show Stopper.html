Advance Wars 2: Black Hole Rising

Show Stopper

Sonja vs. Adder
Difficulty: ****

Mission Briefing: Sonja's intel shows that the Black Hole forces are encamped there. Sonja spots the stronghold but Kanbei refuses to let her go. Sensei convinces Kanbei to let her go. Sonja spots the 8 cannons surrounding the fortress and knows to kill all 8 to win or capture the HQ. Adder laughs, and Sonja quickly notices that it's CO Adder. There is a hidden map to the secret lab here, which is the southwesternmost neutral city. It's in FOW, so it's Sonja's specialty. It'll still annoy you a bit though...

  • Sonja Day 1: Build a recon in NE base, and infantry from western two bases.
  • Adder Day 1: He moves two or so bombers, 3 indirect units, 2 medium tanks, 3 tanks, two copters, 4 infantry, and an A-Air (estimate, as this is FOW) east.
  • Sonja Day 2: Move bottom infantry 3W. Move top infantry 2N 1W. Move recon 4NW. Build an APC in the NW base and two other infantry.
  • Adder Day 2: Move every single unit at you.
  • Sonja Day 3: Move SW infantry 3W to start taking port. Move NW infantry 1W to start taking city. Move other bottom infantry 2W 1N, and other infantry 2W into APC. Move APC 3NW and drop north. Move recon 3N. Make recon from NW base, tank from SW base, infantry from NE base.
  • Adder Day 3: Things move at you...none that are near you at all.
  • Sonja Day 4: Finish capturing city and port. Move NW infantry 1W 2N to start taking neutral base. More northern recon 1NW. Move southern recon 5W. Move tank 3NW. Move other two infantry 3W. Move APC 1W. Build A-Air from NW base.
  • Adder Day 4: ADVANCE! Spot bomber in the south, and three recons, two tanks, artillery, and battle copter in the north.
  • Sonja Day 5: Finish capturing the neutral base. Move A-Air 1W 5N. Make A-Air from NW HQ base. Move infantry near APC into it, move APC 2N, and drop south. Move tank 5N. Move easternmost infantry 3W.
  • dder Day 5: Destroy infantry with tank and battle copter, attack recon with battle copter, destroy infantry with bomber.
  • Sonja Day 6: Move southern A-Air 5W 1S to attack bomber. Move northern A-Air 2SW to take out battle copter. Move tank 3N to weaken artillery. Move recon 2N 1W to attack artillery. Move APC 2NE. Build a medium tank from that newly taken base. Move infantry just east of recon 1NW. Build two infantry from two western HQ bases.
  • Adder Day 6: Bomb recon. Battle copter kill infantry. Recon kill recon. Recons attack APC. Artillery runs. Tank attacks A-Air. Injures his units by a little in the process.
  • Sonja Day 7: Move northern 4HP A-Air 3S to take out weakened bomber. Move 9HP southern A-Air 1NE to take out battle copter. Move medium tank 2N to take out recon. Move northern tank 1S to take out recon to 3HP. Move all three southern infantry 1N 2W. Move 3HP APC 1S 4E into forest. Build a tank from northern base.
  • Adder Day 7: Sidewinder. Start taking northern port with infantry, destroy infantry with recon and tank, destroy recon with tank. Move down recons.
  • Sonja Day 8: Counter Break. Move SW 9HP A-Air 3W to attack recon. Move 4HP A-Air 4N to take out recon to 1HP. Move SE 3HP infantry 1W 2N. Move full infantry 1W to join. Move northern tank 1NW to attack infantry taking port. Move medium tank 1S 4W to attack mech. Move southern tank 1W 4S to attack tank. Build tank from NW HQ base. Build A-Air from northern base.
  • Adder Day 8: Destroy full HP infantry with two recons and tank. Weaken A-Air with tank.
  • Sonja Day 9: Move SE tank on base 4N 2W to weaken tank to 1HP. In the north, finish off infantry taking port with tank. Move full HP A-Air on northern base 2W 3S to take out mech. Move medium tank 1S 3W to attack mech. Move northern 4HP A-Air 3W to take out recon. Move southern 4HP A-Air 1N 4E to take out tank. Move 8HP tank 1SW to attack tank. Build tanks in northern and NW bases. Move APC 5W.
  • Adder Day 9: Finish off 3HP APC with recon. Attack A-Air with mech.
  • Sonja Day 10: Move full HP A-Air 4N to decimate the transport copter. Move SE tank 4W to finish off recon. Move 6HP tank 1N 2E to take out tank. Move southern full HP tank 3N 2W to weaken recon. Move medium tank 1E to take out mech, tank on base 1E to take out recon. Build FOUR infantry. Move 1HP A-Air 4E 2S, 2HP A-Air 1E. Move northern tank 1NW.
  • Adder Day 10: Gets bored! :)
  • Sonja Day 11: Move northern infantry 3N. Move bottom 3 infantry 3W. Shift southern tank 1N 5W. Build recon from northern base. Roll northern full HP A-Air 3W to spot an APC. Move bottom two A-Airs 6W. Build recon from NW HQ base. Move middle two tanks next to northern base 3NW. Move medium tank 5W to spot an A-Air. Move 6HP tank 2N 4W.
  • Adder Day 11: Attack medium tank and tank with hidden rockets.
  • Sonja Day 12: Move 3HP medium tank 3W to spot hidden rocket. In the north, move 3HP tank 6W to spot APC, artillery, and rocket. Transfer northern tank 4W to attack rocket. Move tank just 2SE of that one 1N 4W to attack artillery. Move A-Air 2N 1W to finish off rocket. Move northern infantry 1N to take port. Move northern recon on northern base 3N 2W. Move southern recon 7W 1S. Move bottom three infantry 3W. Move 6HP tank 1N 2W. Move bottom full HP tank 3N 2W into forest. Move bottom 4HP A-Air 1W (out of range, still). Build a medium tank from NW HQ base. Build APC from northern base.
  • Adder Day 12: Sidewinder. Bomber bombs 3HP tank to rubble, destroys A-Air with A-Air. Attack A-Air to 7HP with minicannon. Destroy medium tank with medium tank.
  • Sonja Day 13: Move 7HP A-Air 2W to attack bomber. Finish taking northern port. Move northern tank 1W to attack artillery, use other tank to finish it. Move northern recon 4W. Move APC 4N 2W. In the south, move full HP tank 3E to attack A-Air. Move 4HP A-Air 2N to attack A-Air. Move 6HP tank 3E 2S to take it out to 1HP. Move southern recon 1N 3W to spot rocket. Move bottom infantry 1N 2W, other two 3N. Move southern medium tank 5W.
  • Adder Day 3: Minicannon blast both A-Airs.
  • Sonja Day 14: SUPER POWER: Counter Break. Build A-Air from northern base, medium tank from NW HQ base. Move 9HP southern tank 3W. Take out 1HP A-Air with 1HP A-Air. Move 6HP tank 2W. Start taking the two cities and SW base near you. Move southern medium tank 1N 4W. Move northern full HP tank 1S 3W to attack minicannon. Move northern infantry into APC and trek west. Attack minicannons with other units and move westward.
  • Adder Day 14: Minicannon blast tank and APC, take out two 1HP A-Airs.
  • Sonja Day 15: Finish taking two cities and base. Attack rocket with tank, medium tank with medium tank. Move back units west, attack minicannons with the rest.
  • Adder Day 15: Minicannon blast tank, attack infantry with medium tank.
  • Sonja Day 16: Continue attacking medium tank with medium tank. Use two weakened tanks and one recon to eliminate southern enemy tank. Move units west. Build medium tank from new SW base.
  • Adder Day 16: Sideslip. Attack tank with bomber in the north, attack 4HP infantry with medium tank.
  • Sonja Day 17: Continue attacking minicannons, take out bomber with A-Air. Take out 3HP medium tank with 6HP medium tank. Continue slapping eastern four minicannons. Build a medium tank from SW base.
  • Adder Day 17: Minicannon blast tank. Destroy tank and weaken APC with northern battle copter and hidden rocket.
  • Sonja Day 18: Use new medium tanks in the SE area to eliminate the NE of the two SE cannons. Take out northern battle copter with A-Air. Stay out of minicannon range with all as you keep swiping those minicannons. Also watch out for that SW artillery. Keep moving SW base medium tanks towards the minicannons. Create medium tank from SW base.
  • Adder Day 18: Take out APC with hidden rocket.
  • Sonja Day 19: Take out SE of NE minicannons as well as other SE minicannon. Move 1HP northern tank west to spot APC. Keep attacking minicannons. Move up another medium tank and build another one. Take cities you cleared from SE minicannon area. Take out NE-north minicannon. Move in towards NW minicannons but do not attack it, as it is in rocket range. Do not move A-Air unless it was attacked. Make medium tank.
  • Adder Day 19: Attack medium tank with a battle copter in the south.
  • Sonja Day 20: Destroy 1HP artillery with 3HP tank. Take out both minicannons in SW side. Keep moving medium tanks northwest. Move infantry west as well. Attack NW minicannons with all you have, even in minicannon range, taking out NW-north minicannon in the process.
  • Adder Day 20: Destroy weakened tank, and weaken infantry and recon with battle copters and rocket.
  • Sonja Day 21: Weaken NW minicannon, but DO NOT DESTROY IT! Build A-Air from ALL BASES. Move units to rocket+APC hiding place and move all infantry west. Your goal is here to capture that SW city that BH has, which has the hidden lab map.
  • Adder Day 21: Weaken medium tank with rocket.
  • Sonja Day 22: DESTROY ROCKET. Move A-Air south to destroy battle copter on BH city. Counter Break. Start capturing that SW lab city with full HP infantry. Surround it with 4 units (on all 4 sides, one unit). Take out rocket. Use a weakened medium tank to weaken NW minicannon but not destroy it. Move back units west. Make five infantry and one cruiser from southern port.
  • Adder Day 22: Minicannon blast medium tank.
  • Sonja Day 23: Finish taking SW lab city. "Commander! This was found when we captured a new base." "Ah! It's a map! A map showing the location of their lab! This must be where they're developing those new weapons we've heard about. Please deliver this to my father! He will send reinforcements for sure." "Yes, ma'am! I'll deliver it to CO Kanbei right away!" "Now, I just have to do something with this mess." Take out battle copter with A-Air and finish off NW minicannon to win!
  • End:

Adder is surprised, but still blames it on bad luck. Adder is forced to retreat, and you win by clearing the fortress! Kanbei is impressed with Sonja's ability as well. "Perfect planning wins the day!"

Victory! 99 Speed + 100 Power + 76 Technique = 275 Pts. = A-Rank

Now with three missions with ****, and a lab with ***** in the northeast, you have little options. Choose the southeast mission.
